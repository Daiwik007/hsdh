Gadget Showcase

Amazfit Active 2

The Amazfit Active 2 is a budget-friendly smartwatch with premium features at just $99, offering precision sensors for fitness tracking, 24/7 health monitoring, and up to 10 days of battery life on a single charge. It offers smooth, responsive experience with essential apps and watch faces

Roborock Saros 10

The Saros 10 is a powerful, automated vacuum with a 180-minute runtime, capable of cleaning even the largest homes. It integrates with the Roborock App, allowing users to schedule cleanings, customize patterns, and monitor progress. Its low-height design allows it to reach under furniture, preventing collisions and falls.

Samsung Galaxy XR

Samsung's Galaxy XR mixed reality headset offers immersive virtual and augmented reality experiences for $1,800. Built on the Android XR platform, it seamlessly blends digital content with the real world, allowing for entertainment, productivity, and creative expression. Coming Q4 2025, it will shape the future of digital interaction.

OnePlus Watch 3

The OnePlus Watch 3 is a smartwatch with a 100-hour battery life, perfect for Android users and fitness enthusiasts. It runs on WearOS 5 and offers access to Google ecosystem, including Assistant, Maps, and third-party apps. It tracks workouts, heart rate, sleep patterns, and keeps you connected with notifications and messages..
